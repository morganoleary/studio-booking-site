Class Descriptions

Mat Pilates for Stabilization:

30 minutes

Each individual exercise is done on the mat and can be modified as needed. We will introduce the basics of Pilates breath, alignment, and core principles. If you are looking for a foundation for stabilization and strength that will carry over into everyday life, this is the class for you. Can be paired with Mat Pilates Flow.

Mat Pilates Flow:

45 minutes

We will combine more advanced mat Pilates with other body work exercises for increased strength, coordination, and flow. Breath and grounding will be important aspects of this class as we progress through each phase of movement. Can be paired with Mat Pilates for Stabilization.

Strength & Stretch Movement:

60 minutes

This class will combine a variety of elements: a core strength warm-up, barre exercises, full body engagement exercises done on and off the mat, and deep stretching.
