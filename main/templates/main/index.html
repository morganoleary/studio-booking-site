About Soul Base Pilates

Hello everyone! I am so thankful to be hosting classes at Studio IV and looking forward to all the wonderful possibilities for my class program, Soul Base. This is the start of a journey I have dreamed, plotted, and schemed in my mind for many years, and I’m so excited to be able to share this fresh beginning with all of you (people who value a healthy lifestyle). My background is in professional dance and performance. I have a B.A in Dance from Loyola Marymount University (Los Angeles), studied at the Ailey School in New York City, and traveled with Holland America Cruise Line as a performer in their production shows as well as an instructor for ABC’s Dancing with the Stars at Sea. I have danced commercially as well as in the company context and have always been passionate about keeping a high level of technical training behind it all. That is an aspect of my dance career I will never let go of. I received my certification in Stott Pilates and that has been a great asset to my own strength maintenance as well as the clients I have worked with. This is a big part of “Step One” in my plan for Soul Base. This idea of strength and alignment will be the foundation of how the program grows, and we will build on it. There are many different class styles and resources I’d like to offer for whoever is interested (more to come on that) and this is the very first steppingstone. Now, an important note about Mat Pilates: It is for ANYONE. As you will see in the class descriptions, there are two levels. One is more basic, for stabilization and learning the exercises, and the other will move beyond the basics into faster-paced movement through the whole system. You can pair them up or take just one level consistently- whatever is best for you! Here are some benefits of these classes:  Increased stability and core strength  Improved alignment and posture  Increased flexibility  Improved coordination and balance  Helps to prevent injury and increase body awareness Words cannot express how excited I am for you to join me for Soul Base classes.
